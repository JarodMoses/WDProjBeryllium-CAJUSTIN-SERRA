    DAVAI

    Davai plays the role of Sentinel for PRX, serving as the backbone of the team's defense. On the defenders' side, it is his role to anchor bombsites and make it difficult for enemies to enter. On offense, Davai takes a more strategic role. He secures the flank and lurks for information, providing valuable intel that greatly helps the team execute their entries.
