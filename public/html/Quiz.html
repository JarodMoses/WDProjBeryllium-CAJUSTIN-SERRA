    • Paper Rex Quiz: Which Rex are you?


    Answer 10 questions, about you in this Paper Rex Quiz. After answering all 10, you will be able to see which Rex resembles you the most. Be honest with your answers and enjoy the experience as you find out your Rex!

    1. What role do you usually play?

    2. Your team is hitting a site. What do you do?

    3. How would teammates describe your playstyle?

    4. You’re in a clutch situation (1v2). What’s your mindset?

    5. What’s your biggest strength in Valorant?

    6. If your plan fails mid-round, what do you do?

    7. Which situation excites you the most?

    8. What kind of mistakes do you forgive most (in yourself)?

    9. How do you handle pressure in important matches?

    10. What matters more to you?

    11. Your team is in a numbers disadvantage, what do you do?

    Please answer all the items.

    Your Player is.... Jing!

    You're bold, fearless, and kill hungry.
    Like Jing, you dive into challenges headfirst, and arent afraid to make big plays that can turn the tide of the game. You thrive in high pressure situations, trusting your aim to win all the duels you take. In times of clutch, people look to you for action, and with your raw aim, you can single handedly save the game.

    Your player is.... Forsaken!

    You're the ultimate flex player; adaptive, creative, and unpredictable. Like Forsaken, you are comfortable switching roles based on what your team lates. You trust your instincts and aren't afraid to make risky plays in order to give your team the advantage. You are able to find your way through sticky situations, and your strength is versatility with confidence.

    Your player is.... Something!

    You're the duelist, and most mechanically gifted in your stack, balancing aggression with control. You are the player enemies fear when last alive, as you seldom overextend. Like something, you don't fold under pressure, and stay composed, ensuring clean aim and smart positioning. You aren't always the loudest or flashiest, but your team can always rely on you, and you always deliver.

    Your player is.... Patmen!

    You fill the initiator role of the team, prioritizing gathering info, and setting up the team for their entry. In-game, you value timing, coordination, and util usage more than anything else. Your strong points are your smart util usage, gamesense, and impact for the team. Like Patmen, when playing the game, you see the bigger picture, which allows you to control the pace of the game, and allows your team to keep momentum the whole way through.

    Your player is.... Davai!

    You play sentinel, and are the backbone of the team.
    You value positioning, consistency, and patience above all. You're in charge of anchoring sites, watching flank, and denying openings the enemy could utilize. Like d4v4i, you excel in holding down space, never letting opponents through. While your role doesn't give you many opportunities to take the spotlight, your presence allows the team to play aggressively, without needing to worry about timings or flanks, and your impact is felt through reliability and smart defensive play.
