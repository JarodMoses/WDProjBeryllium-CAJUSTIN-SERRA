    Paper Rex Quiz: Which Rex are you?


    Answer 10 questions about yourself in this Paper Rex Quiz. After completing all 10, you'll discover which Rex resembles you the most. Be honest with your answers and enjoy the experience as you find out your Rex!

    1. What role do you usually play?

    2. Your team is hitting a site. What do you do?

    3. How would teammates describe your playstyle?

    4. You’re in a clutch situation (1v2). What’s your mindset?

    5. What’s your biggest strength in Valorant?

    6. If your plan fails mid-round, what do you do?

    7. Which situation excites you the most?

    8. What kind of mistakes do you forgive most (in yourself)?

    9. How do you handle pressure in important matches?

    10. What matters more to you?

    11. Your team is in a numbers disadvantage, what do you do?

    Please answer all the items.
