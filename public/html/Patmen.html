    Patmen

    Patmen plays as an initiator and controller for PRX. As an initiator, his role is to provide his team with information so they can make the right decisions. Using his utility, he scans the map and gives his team valuable intel. As a controller, his role is to provide cover for his team as they try to take space. Out of the 5 rexes, Patmen is the most controlled and composed player, often saving his teammates from making reckless plays.
