    Jing

    Jing plays as another Duelist for Paper Rex. As a duelist, his role is to create space for his team by entering sites alongside Something. He is known as the "Crazy Raze Player," playing quickly and sometimes recklessly on both offense and defense. However, his aim backs up his aggressive style, as he wins many of his duels, consistently giving his team a strong advantage.
